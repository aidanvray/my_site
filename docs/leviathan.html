In my Game 420 class, one of the projects was the opportunity to simply create a complete game within the given time frame. After a couple weeks of work I ended up making Leviathan, a board game about exploring uncharted ocean. The board is randomly generated and all the tiles start out hidden each game. Players have to explore the board to gradually reveal tiles of the map in their hunt for ancient ocean life. While fishing or hunting players will always run the risk of encountering one of the many deadly leviathans that lurk below, causing them to lose out on their chance to score.
